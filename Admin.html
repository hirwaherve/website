Animal Capturing

An "animal capturing photo moment" is the perfect instant when an animal is
caught in an unposed, natural action, like a bird mid-flight or a deer in a sunlit
clearing. It captures the essence of the animal's behavior and environment, often
using techniques like fast shutter speeds or long lenses to maintain authenticity
without disturbance.
